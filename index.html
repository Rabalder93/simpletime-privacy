Privacy Policy for SimpleTime

Last updated: 4 December 2025

1. Overview

SimpleTime (“the app”) is provided by Joakim Enmalm (“the developer”). This privacy policy explains how the app handles user data. SimpleTime is designed to operate without collecting any personal information.

2. No Data Collection

The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics

All data created within the app is stored locally on the user’s device and never leaves it.

3. Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party services that collect user data, such as:

  • Analytics tools
  • Advertising networks
  • Cloud storage providers
  • Crash reporting services

SimpleTime operates fully offline.

4. Children’s Privacy

Since the app does not collect any personal information, it is safe for users of all ages, including children under the age of 13.

5. Security

No personal information is processed by the app. All app data remains on the user’s device, minimizing any risk of unauthorized access.

6. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the same location as this document.
